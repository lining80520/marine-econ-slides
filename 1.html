Understanding Demand in Marine Economics

What is Demand?

Demand = Quantity consumers are willing and able to purchase at different prices

Key components: Willingness + Ability to pay

Marine Demand Examples

Seafood Consumption
Marine Tourism
Shipping Services

Law of Demand

Price ↑ → Quantity Demanded ↓
Price ↓ → Quantity Demanded ↑

Inverse relationship between price and quantity demanded

Market Equilibrium in Marine Economics

What is Market Equilibrium?

Market Equilibrium = Quantity Supplied = Quantity Demanded

Market forces are balanced

Real Marine Markets

Global Tuna: $18/kg, 2.5M tons/year

Container Shipping: $3,200/TEU, 180M TEU/year

How Equilibrium Works

Price too high → Surplus → Lower price

Price too low → Shortage → Raise price

Equilibrium → Market clears
